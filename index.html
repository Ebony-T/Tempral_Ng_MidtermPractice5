Benefits

  • It gives you a dopamine release.
  • It helps satisfy cravings.

Healthy food is very nourishing but it’s important that everyone indulges because contrary to popular belief, there are many benefits to eating junk food. Specifically, its helpful for one’s mental health. Junk food can bring a great deal of comfort to those going through a hard time, or a great way to treat oneself when celebrating achievements. Coupling this with its general convenience and affordability, junk food is the way to go!
